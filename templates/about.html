About IET On Campus
UET Lahore

IET On Campus UET Lahore has a well-thought and defined structure that emulates the true professional working experience while stimulating the legends like teamwork, Coordination, Time & project management, management & communication skills.

Since its inception; The IET On Campus UET Lahore has been providing an opportunity for maturing engineers to strut their stuff. Born in 2002, with the motto of promoting technology, scientific thinking and innovation, IET On Campus UET Lahore today is a body working for the betterment of professional abilities of students under the patronage of Prof. Tahir Izhar (Society Counsellor and Dean of Electrical Engineering Department, UET Lahore).
